ADEN RAO

Hi, my name is Aden Rao and I'm the 16-year-old kid who made this website. You may know me from my TikTok @genzmoneyy. I have always been interested in trading/investing so I thought why don't I combine my passion for programming with my interest with stocks and make this website that lets you see a very simple view of the largest companies by market cap. I first thought of this idea when I saw another website that was doing this but that website only updated once a day and in my opinion, was too complicated so I decided to make this website which does one thing and one thing only find the top 10 companies by market cap and tells you their market capitalization.
